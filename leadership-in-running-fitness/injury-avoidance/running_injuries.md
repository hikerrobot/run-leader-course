# Injury types

## Muscle injury

- pull or strain
- sore muscles
- tight muscles
- cuts and scrapes

### sore muscles

Likely to last 1 or 2 days after exercise.
Can lead to pull or strain if not enough rest taken.

### tight muscles

Occurs from not cooling down properly or not stretching properly.

## Joint injuries

- sprains
- dislocation

## other types

- bone injury
- inflammation
- achilles tendon pain. excessive exercise
- blisters

## ways to avoid injury

- vary sessions to address all fitness elements
- refer runners to shoe experts
- work on one thing at a time
- suggest running log
- develop runners progressively
- ensure fundamentals are in place
- risk assess routes
