# Cool down

- Accelerates recovery
- Prevents muscle soreness
- Cools the body
- Calms the mind
- Provides time for reflection

Slowly returns body to pre-exercise state.

Example of things to include in cool down:
- Gentle jog and/or walk
- Static stretching of all muscle groups
- Hold stretches for 10-15 seconds
- Session reflection

## Benefits

- Reduces lactic acid build up
