# Performance factors

Things to aim to improve.

## Movement skills

- Agility
- Balance, static and dynamic
- Coordination
- Squats, lunges, push, pull, rotate and hinge

## Lifestyle and support

- Nutrition
- Rest
- Hydration
- Appropriate clothing and equipment
- Awareness of clean athletics

## Physical preparation

- Reaction time
- Strength and conditioning
- Push and pull activities
- Core strength and trunk control


## Technical and tactical

- Specific skill development
- Technique work and practice
- Running at different paces
- Tactics and race or event strategies

## Psycho-behavioural

- Mental strength and resilience
- Dealing with success and failure/winning and not winning
- Internal pressures and external expectations
- Focus of attention and mental rehearsals
