# Equality and Diversity policy

A policy should include: -
- A statement about the club's commitment to the principles and practice of E&D
- A statement encouraging E&D and promoting a inclusion culture
- Guidance on what training more involved club members should access
- What preventative measures the club will/has put in place to stop exclusion
- How incidents will be dealt with
- How to get additional support

The policy should be owned by someone.
It should be a living document and treated as such.
